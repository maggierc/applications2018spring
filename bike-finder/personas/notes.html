Persona:

  1. goals they have
  2. interests — when / why they're buying
  3. knowledge about bikes
  4. limitations — purchasing ability / time available
  5. personality — values / emotions? / attitudes?
  6. context — age / location / job / education?
  7. humanizing — name / photo

Personas can...

  • help put clients in the customer's shoes
  • helps designers to find the important distinctions between different market segments
  • helpful reference during the project, especially with unfamiliar personas to us
  • becomes a helpful reference point after completion (for you/your team)
